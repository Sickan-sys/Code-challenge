Mob Programming

So, what is Mob programming, and how does it work? You will find answers to these questions below.

  • When three or more poeple work on one computer together. They then take on diffrent roles in the team.
  • One person is the Driver, that is the person who's typing on the keyboard, and the only one who makes changes in the code.
  • The other peolpe in the group are Navigators. Since they don't have any typing tp focus on, they can instead discuss and review the code simultaneously as it is being written.
  • Everyone needs to be comfortable asking questions to the hole group, to make shure they know what is being. The goal is that everyone in the group is activly involved in the work.
